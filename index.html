The Oxford University Society of Edinburgh

Find us on Facebook and LinkedIn

Membership of the Edinburgh branch of the Oxford University Society is open to all graduates of the University of Oxford and is aimed at those residing or working in Edinburgh and the surrounding areas of south and central Scotland. We welcome all alumni and their guests to our regular events.

The principle aims of the association are to:
  • Provide a means for graduates to keep in touch with each other
  • Organise activities and social events for alumni within the region
  • Offer opportunities for alumni to network, socialise and offer their skills and expertise for the benefit of the University and other alumni
  • Maintain contact with Oxford and facilitate a two-way dialogue between local alumni and the University
  • Strengthen the relationship between the University and its former students
  • Enhance the image of the University of Oxford on a local and regional level and encourage accessibility
